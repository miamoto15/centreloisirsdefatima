## Accessibility Standards

We aim to comply with the **Web Content Accessibility Guidelines (WCAG) 2.1** at Level AA, an internationally recognized standard. These guidelines explain how to make web content more accessible to people with disabilities, including those with:

- Visual impairments
- Hearing impairments
- Motor limitations
- Cognitive disabilities

## Accessibility Measures

Our website incorporates several features to improve accessibility:

### Navigation

- **Clear and consistent structure**: Logically organized navigation
- **"Skip to content" link**: Allows users to jump directly to main content
- **Keyboard navigation**: All features are accessible via keyboard
- **Focus indicators**: Active elements are clearly identified

### Content

- **Descriptive headings**: Logical heading hierarchy for easier navigation
- **Alternative text**: Descriptions for informative images
- **Explicit links**: Link text clearly describes their destination
- **Sufficient contrast**: Text and backgrounds provide good contrast

### Technology

- **Semantic HTML**: Appropriate tags used for structure
- **Screen reader compatibility**: Optimized for assistive technologies
- **Responsive design**: Site usable on various devices and screen sizes
- **No tracking cookies**: Respect for your privacy

## Known Limitations

Despite our efforts, some aspects of the site may present accessibility challenges:

- Some older images may lack complete alternative descriptions
- Some external or embedded content may not be fully accessible
- We are continuously working to identify and address these limitations

## Technologies Used

This site is built with:

- HTML5
- CSS3
- Minimal JavaScript for essential interactions
- Hugo (static site generator)

The site is designed to work with:

- Recent screen readers (JAWS, NVDA, VoiceOver)
- Modern browsers (Chrome, Firefox, Safari, Edge)
- Screen magnification tools

## Feedback and Support

We welcome your feedback on the accessibility of our website. If you encounter difficulties or accessibility barriers, please contact us by email at **info@centreloisirsfatima.com**.

We will strive to provide you with the information in an accessible format or offer an appropriate alternative.

## Evaluation and Continuous Improvement

We are committed to:

- Regularly assessing the accessibility of our site

- Integrating accessibility from the design phase of new features
- Taking user feedback into account to improve the experience
